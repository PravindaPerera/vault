World Whiskey Society 20 Year Cigar Blend

American Light Whisky Cigar Blend Gothic Edition | Aged 20 Years. Introducing the newest edition to the World Whiskey lineup: American Light Whisky Cigar Blend, made with six different cask finishes and aged for 20 years for your enjoyment. This blend showcases a harmonious interplay of sweetness, spice, and richness, making its deep amber hues and depth of flavor an excellent companion for a fine cigar. On the nose, experience a rich and complex bouquet, with notes of dried fruits, particularly figs and raisins from the PX Sherry and Madeira, balanced by the sweetness of caramel and toffee from the Cognac. Subtle spices from the tequila barrels enhance the aroma, alongside earthy notes reminiscent of aged tobacco leaves. With each sip, a bold, luscious mouthfeel with flavors of dark chocolate and roasted nuts greets the palate. The rich sweetness from Tawny Port and PX Sherry provides depth, while hints of citrus and herbal undernotes from the Awamori add a unique twist. The complexity continues with layers of oak and soft spice harmonizing the blend. The finish is long and warming, with lingering notes of dried fruit, dark caramel, and a hint of smokiness, perfectly complemented by a touch of citrus zest and oak from the barrel aging, leaving a pleasant aftertaste that invites another indulgence. 48% Alc. by Vol.
